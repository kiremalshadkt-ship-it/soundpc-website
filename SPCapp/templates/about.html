About Sound Puppet Comics

Our Story

Sound Puppet Comics is a unique multimedia experience that combines the art of storytelling with the power of sound. Our universe features god-like beings, cosmic protectors, and dark forces that shape reality itself.

What We Offer

  • Interactive Comics: Experience stories that come alive with sound and animation
  • Audiobooks: Immerse yourself in narrated versions of our epic tales
  • Character Deep Dives: Learn about the beings that inhabit our universe
  • Series Exploration: Discover interconnected storylines across multiple dimensions

Our Mission

To create immersive worlds where sound and imagination collide, bringing you entertainment that engages all your senses.
